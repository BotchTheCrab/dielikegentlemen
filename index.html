Die Like Gentlemen
About

Like an unwanted gray hair, Die Like Gentlemen sprung out of the heavy music scene in Portland, Oregon: distinguished, ornery, and unstoppable. Steeped in Sabbathy riffs, progressive arrangements and melodic anger, these whiskey-swilling gentlemen are aiming to spread their sludge across the Pacific Northwest.
